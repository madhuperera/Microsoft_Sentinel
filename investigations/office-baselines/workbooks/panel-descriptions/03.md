### 📈 03. Activity over time

| Workbook context | Value |
|---|---|
| Selected period | `{W_TimeRange:start}` to `{W_TimeRange:end}` |
| Target user filter | `{W_TargetUser}` *(blank means the whole organisation)* |

This is a line chart of activity over time, with one line per Microsoft 365 app. It uses hourly buckets, so a one-day report still shows detail through the day. It helps you see whether a change was gradual or sudden, and which app drove it. Leave the user filter blank for the whole organisation, or set it to focus on one person.

This is an operational trend view. A change may be expected due to known business activity, holidays, maintenance, onboarding, offboarding or service changes.

#### What the chart shows

| Element | What it means |
|---|---|
| Horizontal axis (`TimeGenerated`) | The time bucket the activity happened in (hourly). |
| Vertical axis (`Events`) | The number of activity events counted in that bucket. |
| Lines (`Workload`) | One line per Microsoft 365 app, for example Exchange, SharePoint, OneDrive or Microsoft Teams. |

#### What is included

Everyday staff activity only. Guests, system accounts and listed service accounts are excluded.
