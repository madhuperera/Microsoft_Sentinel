### 🌐 08. The organisation's activity over time

| Workbook context | Value |
|---|---|
| Selected period | `{W_TimeRange:start}` to `{W_TimeRange:end}` |

This is a day-by-day line chart of the whole organisation's activity for each Microsoft 365 app. It is shown as an average per active person, so it is not skewed by how many people happened to be active that day. It is useful for spotting organisation-wide effects such as public holidays or quiet periods.

This is an operational trend view. A change may be expected due to known business activity.

#### What the chart shows

| Element | What it means |
|---|---|
| Horizontal axis (`Day`) | The day. |
| Vertical axis (`AvgEventsPerActiveUser`) | The average number of activity events per active person that day. |
| Lines (`Workload`) | One line per Microsoft 365 app. |

#### What is included

Everyday staff activity only. Guests, system accounts and listed service accounts are excluded.
