### 🌐 08. The organisation's activity over time

| Workbook context | Value |
|---|---|
| Selected period | `{W_TimeRange:start}` to `{W_TimeRange:end}` |

This is a line chart of the whole organisation's activity over time for each Microsoft 365 app, using hourly buckets so a one-day report still shows detail. It is shown as an average per active person, so it is not skewed by how many people happened to be active in each bucket. It is useful for spotting organisation-wide effects such as public holidays or quiet periods.

This is an operational trend view. A change may be expected due to known business activity.

#### What the chart shows

| Element | What it means |
|---|---|
| Horizontal axis (`TimeBin`) | The time bucket (hourly). |
| Vertical axis (`AvgEventsPerActiveUser`) | The average number of activity events per active person in that bucket. |
| Lines (`Workload`) | One line per Microsoft 365 app. |

#### What is included

Everyday staff activity only. Guests, system accounts and listed service accounts are excluded.
