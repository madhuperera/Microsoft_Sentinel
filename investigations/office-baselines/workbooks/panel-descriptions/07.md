### ⚖️ 07. Each person compared with the organisation

| Workbook context | Value |
|---|---|
| Selected period | `{W_TimeRange:start}` to `{W_TimeRange:end}` |
| Minimum activity to be counted | `{W_MinUserActivity}` events in the period |
| Unusual mark | Flagged when well outside the typical range (setting `{W_OutlierZ}`) |
| Target user filter | `{W_TargetUser}` *(blank means all users)* |

This view places each person against the wider organisation for each app, and flags the genuinely unusual cases. The organisation's typical level uses the median (the middle person), which is less affected by a few very high or very low users than a simple average.

This is supporting context, not a league table. Different roles naturally sit in different places, so treat a flag as a question, not a finding.

#### Column guide

| Column | What it means |
|---|---|
| `UserDisplayName` | The person's display name. |
| `UPN` | The user principal name, usually their sign-in address. |
| `Workload` | The Microsoft 365 app. |
| `UserEventsPerDay` | This person's average events per day in this app during the selected period. |
| `GroupMedian` | The organisation's typical events per day for this app (the median). |
| `PercentileBand` | Where the person sits compared with everyone else (see below). |
| `ModifiedZ` | How far from typical the person is, on a robust scale. Around `0` is typical; larger numbers, positive or negative, are more unusual. |
| `OrgFlag` | Whether the person is flagged as unusual (see below). |

#### Where they sit (`PercentileBand`)

| `PercentileBand` | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `Below P25 (low vs peers)` | Among the lower-activity quarter. |
| `P25-P50` | Below the typical level, but not in the lowest quarter. |
| `P50-P75` | Above the typical level. |
| `P75-P90` | In the higher-activity range. |
| `Above P90 (high vs peers)` | Among the most active one in ten. |

#### Flag (`OrgFlag`)

| `OrgFlag` | Meaning | Action |
|---|---|---|
| `Typical` | Within the normal range for the organisation. | No action needed. |
| `Outlier - review only` | Well outside the normal range. | Worth a look in business context. |

#### What is included

Everyday staff activity. People below `{W_MinUserActivity}` events are left out of the comparison. Guests, system accounts and listed service accounts are excluded.

#### How rows are sorted

Rows are grouped by app, then show the most unusual high-activity people first.
